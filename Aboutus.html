About Us


Integrow is a next-generation, tech-driven real estate investment

platform. Our investment products focus on institutional-grade real

estate that has traditionally been opaque & inaccessible to individual

as well as institutional investors. Our current offerings focus on

residential and commercial real estate.


Ram Yadav, founded the company in a strategic partnership with

Aurum PropTech Ltd., a NSE listed company with a mission to

mainstreaming real estate as a core asset class. Aurum focuses on

developing cutting edge technologies for the real estate industry.
